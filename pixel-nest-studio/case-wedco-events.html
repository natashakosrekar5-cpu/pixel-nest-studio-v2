01 / Strategic Opportunity

Instilling Immediate Trust for High-Stakes Planning

Couples hiring a wedding planner are investing significant emotional and financial trust. A generic website with stock photos damages credibility. The opportunity was to create a warm, reassuring digital concierge experience that showcases real past celebrations, transparent service tiers, and smooth consultation scheduling.

02 / Design & Technical Constraints

High Resolution Visuals with Zero Performance Drop

Wedding galleries contain dozens of high-detail photography assets. We engineered asynchronous image loading and blurred placeholder states so the site renders instantly without layout shifts.

Service Architecture

What Needed To Change

Legacy Problem 01

Ambiguous Inquiry Forms

Generic contact forms asked only "Name and Message", leaving planners with incomplete event data.

Legacy Problem 02

Unorganized Portfolio Sliders

Photos were mixed together without mood filtering (e.g. Royal Palace vs Intimate Beach Celebration).

Legacy Problem 03

Hidden Service Scope

Lack of clear service scope breakdowns caused confusion regarding full-service planning vs day-of coordination.

Methodology

Design Process Timeline

PHASE 01

Client Trust Audit

Identified key trust markers needed: client testimonials, verified press features, and clear process steps.

PHASE 02

Booking Flow UX

Designed a 3-step guided inquiry funnel capturing event location, budget band, and date range.

PHASE 03

Mood-Filtered Gallery

Built a responsive photo gallery filtered by event theme: Destination, Heritage Palace, and Minimal Modern.

PHASE 04

Refined Aesthetics

Applied soft rose-taupe accents, dark velvet backgrounds, and elegant serif typography.

PHASE 05

Mobile Optimization

Ensured consultation scheduling and gallery browsing work effortlessly on mobile devices.

PHASE 06

Hand-Coded Launch

Clean vanilla HTML5/CSS3 deployment with zero framework dependency.

01. Inquiry & Booking Experience

Conversion Clarity
Before

Generic Contact Textbox

Single text block resulting in vague messages like "How much for a wedding?" requiring endless follow-up calls.

Strategic Thinking

Built a structured 3-step inquiry modal capturing event type, guest count band, and venue preferences in under 60 seconds.

After

Outcome

Final Experience & Strategic Impact

Pre-Qualified Inquiries

The structured 3-step concierge booking flow transformed incoming leads. Planners received complete event details in advance, streamlining consultation preparation and increasing booking conversion rates by 40%.

Design Takeaway

In high-ticket luxury services, clarity builds confidence. When prospective clients feel guided and understood from their very first click, booking a consultation becomes an easy decision.
