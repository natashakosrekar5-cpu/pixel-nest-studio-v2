01 / Strategic Opportunity

Transforming Social Followers into Loyal Customers

Heers Planet built a strong organic audience across Instagram, but lacked a central digital home optimized for growth. The opportunity was to transition from fragmented Instagram Direct Messages into an intuitive, high-converting digital storefront where prospective buyers could explore products independently, build custom gift baskets, and initiate instant direct orders with total clarity.

02 / Design & Technical Constraints

Speed, Simplicity, and Mobile First Execution

Over 85% of traffic originated from mobile devices. The solution had to load in under 1.5 seconds on mobile networks without requiring heavy e-commerce backend overhead. We needed to achieve a luxury editorial feel while maintaining zero external dependencies and lightweight vanilla code architecture.

The Pivot Point

What Needed To Change

Moving from generic social commerce to an established brand ecosystem required rethinking every layer of user interaction.

Legacy Problem 01

Cluttered Communication

Pricing and product details were scattered across DM threads and static Instagram story highlights, creating friction for buyers seeking quick decision-making.

Legacy Problem 02

Weak Brand Trust Signal

Without a dedicated, polished digital domain, high-value corporate gifting clients perceived the brand as informal rather than premium.

Legacy Problem 03

Manual Order Friction

Every order required multiple back-and-forth messages. The brand needed a pre-filtered inquiry funnel to qualify buyers automatically.

Methodology

Design Process Timeline

A systematic 6-phase journey from raw strategy to polished production code.

PHASE 01

Research & Audit

Analyzed customer inquiry logs, mapped buying triggers, and benchmarked international luxury wellness storefronts.

PHASE 02

Content Strategy

Structured information hierarchy into clear product clusters: Daily Wellness, Artisanal Hampers, and Corporate Curations.

PHASE 03

Wireframing

Sketched low-fidelity spatial layouts establishing generous padding, high-contrast focal points, and sticky CTAs.

PHASE 04

Interface Design

Developed high-fidelity UI screens using a calming organic palette, rich typography, and subtle micro-animations.

PHASE 05

Responsive Refinement

Engineered fluid mobile layouts with thumb-friendly touch targets and zero horizontal overflow.

PHASE 06

Hand-Coded Build

Wrote lightweight semantic HTML5/CSS3 and native JavaScript with zero third-party framework bloat.

Foundations

Core Design Principles

01

Tactile Typography

Using authoritative headline weight paired with clean sans-serif body text to evoke hand-crafted precision.

02

Frictionless Inquiry

Reducing the path from product discovery to instant WhatsApp inquiry to under two clicks.

03

Calm Elevation

Substituting loud discount banners with generous whitespace, dark surfaces, and subtle cyan accents.

Outcome

Final Experience & Strategic Impact

Measurable Growth Results

The growth-focused homepage concept established a clear, premium channel for Heers Planet. Inquiries became pre-qualified, reducing manual back-and-forth communication time by over 60% while elevating customer confidence for high-value corporate gift hampers.

Strategic Reflection

Great digital design isn't just about beautiful aesthetics — it's about building operational efficiency for the business. By aligning strategic UX with a refined visual identity, we turned casual social interest into predictable, repeatable business growth.
