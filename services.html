How we work

Eight steps. No surprises.

The same process on every project, so you always know what's next and why.

01
Discovery

We learn your business, your customers, and what the site actually needs to do for you.

02
Strategy

Before any visuals, we map the pages, the goal of each one, and the customer's path through the site.

03
Wireframing

A structural layout of each page — no color or type decisions yet, just what goes where and why.

04
Design

A homepage concept built around your brand — you see it before anything is finalized.

05
Development

We build it fully responsive and fast, with real content — not placeholder text.

06
Testing

Checked across devices and browsers, with load speed and broken links caught before you ever see them.

07
Launch

Your site goes live, connected to your domain and ready for real customers.

08
Support

A walkthrough so you're comfortable making small updates yourself, and we stay reachable after that too.

Why strategy comes before visuals: a beautiful page that leads customers nowhere is just decoration. We map the path first, then design around it.

Common questions

FAQ

How long does a project take?
Most single-page sites are ready in 3–5 days. Multi-page sites with more content or custom features usually take 7–10 days.
Do I need to provide content and photos?
Ideally yes — your own photos and details make the site feel authentic. If you don't have them ready, we'll help you figure out what's needed and can work with placeholders until you do.
Can I update the site myself later?
Yes. We walk you through how to make simple text and image updates after launch, and we're always reachable for anything bigger.
What if I need something not listed here?
Just ask. Most requests — booking systems, galleries, multilingual pages — are possible; we'll tell you honestly if something isn't a good fit.

Not sure which service you need?

Tell us about your business and we'll recommend the right starting point.
